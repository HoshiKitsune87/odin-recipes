Omurice

The omurice dish

Omurice is a japanese dish consisting of an omelette made with fried rice and thin, scrambled eggs, usually topped with ketchup. It is a popular dish also commonly cooked at home.

Ingredients consist of:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil

For chicken ketchup recipe

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 150g of chicken breast
  • half onion
  • half a medium carrot
  • 1/4 cup of frozen green peas
  • 2 tbsp of ketchup
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make Omurice

  1. Chop the chicken, onion, and capsicum into small cubes.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over high heat.
  3. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until the meat has all turned white
  4. Add the vegetables and cook until the onion becomes translucent.
  5. Add the ketchup and mix it all together.
  6. Add the rice and mix until the rice is all coated with the ketchup.
  7. Add thawed frozen peas to the ketchup rice and mix them together.
  8. Flavor with salt and pepper.
  9. Remove from the heat and set aside in a large bowl.
  10. In a small bowl crack and beat 2 eggs.
  11. Heat the large frying pan with 1/2 tbsp of olive oil over medium heat and pour in the egg, allowing it to spread all over the bottom frying pan and slightly scramble the middle withbreaking the omelette.
  12. Turn off the heat, and pour half the ketchup rice into the center of the omelette.
  13. Push the omelette and rice to one side of the pan and fold in the sides of the omelette to wrap the rice.
  14. Hold a plate beside the omelette and tip the pan a bit to slide the omelette onto the plate so that it flips on to the plate so the egg is on top covering the rice.
  15. Repeat these steps again for the next 3 eggs and remaining chicken rice then serve.

Enjoy!
